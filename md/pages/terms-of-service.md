# Terms of Service Page Plan

## 1. Page Purpose
Establish the legal terms and conditions for using the Synex Digital website and services.

## 2. Target User Intent
Review legal obligations, limitations of liability, and service agreements.

## 3. Recommended Route
`/terms-of-service` (Handled by `app/(site)/terms-of-service/page.tsx`)

## 4. Page Sections
1. **Header:** "Terms of Service" with last updated date.
2. **Content Blocks:** Acceptance of terms, Intellectual Property, Limitation of Liability, Governing Law, etc.

## 5. Section-by-Section Content Guidance
- Clear hierarchical text structure.

## 6. Suggested UI Components
- `<LegalLayout>` (Shared with Privacy Policy).

## 7. Animation and Interaction Guidance
- Minimal.

## 8. Image Usage Guidance
- None.

## 9. CTA Strategy
- None.

## 10. SEO Metadata Suggestions
- **Title:** `Terms of Service | Synex Digital`
- (Optional `noindex` as with Privacy Policy).

## 11. Database Content Requirements
- None. Fully static.

## 12. Responsive Design Behavior
- Ensure comfortable reading width on desktop.

## 13. Development Notes for Next.js and Tailwind
- Utilize the same `@tailwindcss/typography` implementation as the Privacy Policy for rapid development.

## 14. Reusable Components Needed
- `LegalContainer`
